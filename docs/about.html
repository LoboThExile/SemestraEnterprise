About Semestra Enterprise

Preserving and promoting Malaysian culture through educational products

Our Mission

Semestra Enterprise is a small business dedicated to making learning Malaysian culture fun and accessible. We create educational products that help people, especially younger audiences, appreciate and understand the rich heritage of Malaysia and Southeast Asia.

Our Products

Our flagship product, Malaysian Heritage Match, is a card-based educational game that brings Malaysian and Southeast Asian culture to life. It's designed to be both entertaining and informative, perfect for students, families, and casual players.

Our Values

  • Cultural Appreciation: We believe in the importance of understanding and appreciating diverse cultures.
  • Educational Excellence: Our products are designed to be both fun and educational.
  • Accessibility: We aim to make cultural learning accessible to everyone.
  • Quality: We are committed to creating high-quality, engaging products.
